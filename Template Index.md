# Amazon Working Backwards Framework - Template Index

Quick access to all 7 strategic planning templates.

---

## Core Product Development Framework

### 1. PR-FAQ (Press Release with FAQ)
**Start here first!**

📄 **Template**: `[[Amazon Working Backwards Framework - PR-FAQ]]`

**Purpose**: Define customer experience and validate idea
**When to use**: Before any other documents, at idea stage
**Time**: 2-3 hours first draft, 5-10+ iterations
**Key question**: "Would customers be excited about this?"

**Creates**: Press release + 5-10 FAQs (500-700 words total)

---

### 2. PRD (Product Requirements Document)

📄 **Template**: `[[Amazon Working Backwards Framework - PRD]]`

**Purpose**: Specify exactly what to build and how it works
**When to use**: After PR-FAQ is approved
**Time**: 1-2 days first draft
**Key question**: "What are we building?"

**Creates**: Product specification with features, requirements, metrics

---

### 3. MRD (Marketing Requirements Document)

📄 **Template**: `[[Amazon Working Backwards Framework - MRD]]`

**Purpose**: Define market-driven requirements (what customers need)
**When to use**: Before or parallel to PRD
**Time**: 1-2 days
**Key question**: "What does the market need?"

**Creates**: Market requirements + GTM strategy + pricing

---

## Supporting Strategic Analysis

### 4. Competitive Analysis

📄 **Template**: `[[Amazon Working Backwards Framework - Competitive Analysis]]`

**Purpose**: Understand competitive landscape and define differentiation
**When to use**: Before PR-FAQ to inform positioning
**Time**: 2-3 days
**Key question**: "How do we win?"

**Creates**: Competitor profiles, SWOT analysis, strategic positioning

---

### 5. Market Analysis

📄 **Template**: `[[Amazon Working Backwards Framework - Market Analysis]]`

**Purpose**: Validate market opportunity and business viability
**When to use**: Before committing resources
**Time**: 3-5 days
**Key question**: "Is this market big enough?"

**Creates**: TAM/SAM/SOM, unit economics, GO/NO-GO recommendation

---

### 6. M&A Analysis

📄 **Template**: `[[Amazon Working Backwards Framework - M&A Analysis]]`

**Purpose**: Understand acquisition landscape and plan exit strategy
**When to use**: Before fundraising or long-term planning
**Time**: 2-3 days
**Key question**: "Who would acquire us?"

**Creates**: Acquirer profiles, valuation benchmarks, exit roadmap

---

### 7. Investor Landscape

📄 **Template**: `[[Amazon Working Backwards Framework - Investor Landscape]]`

**Purpose**: Map investor ecosystem and plan fundraising
**When to use**: 6-24 months before raising capital
**Time**: 2-3 days
**Key question**: "Who will fund us?"

**Creates**: Investor targets, valuation benchmarks, fundraising strategy

---

## Quick Start Guide

### For New Product Ideas
1. Start with **PR-FAQ** ✅
2. Validate with **Market Analysis** ✅
3. Check **Competitive Analysis** ✅
4. If compelling → **MRD** → **PRD** ✅

### For Fundraising
1. **Investor Landscape** ✅
2. **Market Analysis** ✅
3. **M&A Analysis** ✅

### For Exit Planning
1. **M&A Analysis** ✅
2. **Market Analysis** ✅
3. **Competitive Analysis** ✅

## How to Use

1. **Copy entire template** into AI chat (Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini)
2. **Provide context** about your product/company
3. **Let AI generate first draft** following template structure
4. **Iterate based on feedback** (expect 5-10+ rounds)
5. **Share with stakeholders** and refine

---

**Remember**: Amazon expects 10+ drafts and 5+ review rounds. First drafts should take hours, not days.
