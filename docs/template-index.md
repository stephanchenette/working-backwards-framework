# Template Index

Quick index of the files in this repo, grouped by role in the system.

## Core Working Backwards Artifacts

### 1. PR/FAQ

**File**: `core/pr-faq.md`  
**Use first**  
**Question answered**: Would a customer actually care about this?

Start here before anything else. If the PR/FAQ does not produce real clarity, do not expand the stack.

### 2. MRD

**File**: `core/mrd.md`  
**Question answered**: What does the market actually need?

Use this to sharpen the market requirements and validate that the product promise is grounded in real demand.

### 3. PRD

**File**: `core/prd.md`  
**Question answered**: What are we actually building?

Use this after the customer and market story are coherent enough to turn into requirements.

## Strategic Extensions

### 4. Market Analysis

**File**: `extensions/market-analysis.md`  
**Question answered**: Is the opportunity large and attractive enough to justify building this?

### 5. Competitive Analysis

**File**: `extensions/competitive-analysis.md`  
**Question answered**: Where do we win, and where are we weak?

### 6. Investor Landscape

**File**: `extensions/investor-landscape.md`  
**Question answered**: Who would plausibly fund this, and what proof points would they expect?

### 7. M&A Analysis

**File**: `extensions/ma-analysis.md`  
**Question answered**: Who would plausibly acquire this, and why?

## Suggested Workflow

### For a new startup idea

1. `core/pr-faq.md`
2. `extensions/market-analysis.md`
3. `extensions/competitive-analysis.md`
4. `core/mrd.md`
5. `core/prd.md`

If the idea still looks strong:

6. `extensions/investor-landscape.md`
7. `extensions/ma-analysis.md`

### For an existing product

Start with:

- `extensions/competitive-analysis.md`
- `extensions/market-analysis.md`
- `core/mrd.md`

Then update:

- `core/pr-faq.md`
- `core/prd.md`

### For fundraising prep

Start with:

- `extensions/investor-landscape.md`
- `extensions/market-analysis.md`

Use the core documents to ensure the story is still coherent.

## Important Distinction

The core documents are closest to classic Working Backwards.

The extension documents are a broader strategic system layered on top of it.
